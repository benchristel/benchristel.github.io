Immortality

A man went to see the sage. He said, "Life is so short and our effect on the world is so limited. How can I be happy knowing that someday I'll disappear without leaving a trace?"

"You're mortal because you're a human," said the sage. "Learn to be something eternal instead."

The man bowed before the sage and later became her disciple. One day as he was meditating by a stream he became the sound of a waterfall.
